_Last Updated: {{privacy_last_updated}}_

Market Memory (“we,” “our,” or “the Service”) values your privacy.

Market Memory is a market report and briefing service that helps users read and review financial market information.  
The Service is currently provided as an MVP and may be updated over time.

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information.

### Account Information

When you create an account or log in to the Service, we may collect:

* Name or display name
* Email address
* Authentication provider information, such as Google login ID
* Account settings and subscription status

### Usage Information

When you use the Service, we may collect:

* Pages viewed
* Reports opened or searched
* Access time
* Device, browser, and operating system information
* IP address
* Basic log data used for security, debugging, and service improvement

### Payment Information

If paid features are introduced, payment processing may be handled by third-party payment providers such as Stripe.

We do not directly store full credit card numbers or payment card details on our own servers.

### Communication Information

If you contact us by email, form, or other communication method, we may collect:

* Your email address
* Your message content
* Information necessary to respond to your inquiry

### Cookies and Similar Technologies

We may use cookies or similar technologies to:

* Keep you logged in
* Maintain basic service functionality
* Understand how the Service is used
* Improve performance and user experience

You can control cookies through your browser settings.
However, disabling cookies may affect some features of the Service.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

* To provide and operate the Service
* To create and manage user accounts
* To authenticate users
* To display reports and related content
* To improve the quality, stability, and usability of the Service
* To analyze usage trends
* To prevent fraud, abuse, unauthorized access, and security incidents
* To respond to inquiries
* To manage subscriptions or payments, if paid features are introduced
* To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information.

## 3. Market Data and AI-Generated Content

Market Memory may use public market data, news, financial indicators,   
and other publicly available information to generate reports, summaries, and briefings.

Unless clearly stated otherwise, personal information provided by users is not used as the main source for generating market reports.

Some report generation, summarization, translation, or classification processes may use external AI service providers.

We may share limited information with third-party service providers only when necessary to operate the Service.

These providers may include:

* Hosting providers
* Database providers
* Authentication providers

These providers are allowed to process information only as necessary to provide their services to us.

We may also disclose information when required by law, regulation, legal process, or government request.

The Service may use cloud services or third-party providers located outside your country or region.

As a result, your information may be processed or stored in countries other than your country of residence.  
When we transfer or process information internationally, we take reasonable steps to protect it in accordance with applicable laws.

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

If you request account deletion, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within a reasonable period, unless we are required to retain it by law or for legitimate business reasons such as security, fraud prevention, or dispute resolution.

You are responsible for keeping your login credentials safe and for using the Service in a secure environment.
